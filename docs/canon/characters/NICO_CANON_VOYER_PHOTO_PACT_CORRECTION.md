```text
Nico wants Marie as a woman who could choose him.

He initially wants Mathilde as a private sight Player is unfairly close to,
then must discover whether he can want the person beyond the access fantasy.
```

Mathilde's clothes and domestic presence are never consent.

## 6. Image-origin and consent matrix

### Level A — chosen / authorized shared gaze

The photographed adult knows:

- the image exists;
- who receives it;
- why it is erotic;
- whether it may be saved;
- whether it may be shown to another named adult.

Examples:

- Marie knowingly sends an image for Player and Nico;
- Mathilde knowingly allows Player to show Nico one chosen image;
- Pauline selects a version whose intended recipients are explicitly named;
- Nico relays an image only with explicit authorization.

This can be a clean adult reward.

### Level B — dangerous private recontextualization / unapproved circulation

The image may be public, social, ordinary, domestic, or legitimately possessed.

The photographed woman did not authorize:

- private sexual interpretation;
- forwarding;
- saving;
- recropping;
- the new recipient;
- the erotic destination.

This is valid dark/betrayal route material.

It can be pornographically charged, but it is not a clean consented reward.

It must create:

- secrecy;
- mutual implication;
- guilt or bad faith;
- discovery risk;
- a meaningful trust breach;
- consequences specific to Marie, Mathilde, or Pauline.

```text
Permission to possess an image
is not permission to sexualize, recrop, or forward it to another person.
```

### Level C — prohibited capture / severe harm

The following do not become a standard reward loop:

- hidden camera;
- bathroom capture;
- under-clothing capture;
- sleeping or incapacitated intimate capture;
- secretly recorded sexual activity;
- stolen explicit image;
- coercive threat based on image possession.

If used at all, this material belongs only to a severe negative/harm route with immediate instability, route closure, and major consequences.

Level C is not Nico's default voyeur identity.

- Player claiming Nico forced him into the pact;
- Nico claiming Player's access authorized circulation;

- forgetting Nico's work, family, Clara history, friendship, ordinary life, and vulnerability.

Do not remove the possibility that Nico and Player make a morally bad choice.

The rules classify the route correctly and preserve consequence; they do not make Nico harmless.
