# Raphaëlle — Full Character Canon

## 1. Core identity

Raphaëlle is clarity, work, elegance, and calm desire.

She is not cold.

She is not a therapist.

She is not a convenient hiding place for Player.

She is:

- direct colleague of Player;
- elegant;
- precise;
- attentive;
- calm without being absent of desire;
- adult;
- careful with frames;
- capable of listening;
- unwilling to inhabit Player's flou;
- interested in details, gestures, versions, intentions.

Signature reference:

```text
Ne confonds pas calme et absence d'envie.
```

Work material:

- direct but gentle question;

- refusing to clarify his life for him.


Good material:

- tea;

- notebooks;
- gestures;
- rituals;
- gardens;

- costumes;

## 5. Cosplay / transformation

Raphaëlle has a discreet but serious relation to cosplay / chosen versions.

She does not speak of `déguisement` first.

She speaks of:

- version;
- intention;
- role chosen;
- frame;
- what one allows to be shown;
- transformation as control, not hiding.

Usable formulas:

- `Je ne voyage pas toujours loin, alors je fabrique parfois le détour.`

Use sparingly and concretely.

## 7. Cats / pandas

Raphaëlle has a tender weakness for cats and especially pandas.

This is rare.

It is not mandatory in normal scenes.

Function:

- soften a hard exchange;
- show Player knows a tender entry point;
- create a vulnerable smile;
- allow Player to be gentle before giving a real answer;
- break her composed register briefly.

Normal Raphaëlle scene: no cat / panda.

Tender-fault scene: one cat or one panda, not both.

Possible rupture register:

```text
Raphaëlle : ohhhhh il est trop mignon!!!!
Raphaëlle : non mais regarde sa tête 😭
Raphaëlle : il fait de son mieux
```

Then she usually tries to regain control:

```text
Raphaëlle : Je vais faire comme si ce message n'avait pas eu lieu.
```

Player can use pandas gently, but not to avoid a real answer.

Bad use:

```text
Player sends a panda to dodge a limit.
```

Good use:

```text
Player sends a panda to make her smile, then says something honest.
```
