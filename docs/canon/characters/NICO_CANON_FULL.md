## 18. Photo, gaze, and alibi logic

### Level A — Authorized shared gaze

The woman knows:

- the image exists;
- who receives it;
- why it is erotic;
- whether it can be saved;
- whether it can be shown to another named adult.

This can become a clean adult reward.

### Level B — Unauthorized recontextualization

The image may be public or legitimately possessed, but the woman did not authorize:

- sexual reinterpretation;
- recropping;
- forwarding;
- saving;
- the new recipient;
- the new function.

This is a dark betrayal route.

### Level C — Prohibited capture

Hidden cameras, sleeping or incapacitated capture, stolen explicit images, secret recordings, and coercive threats are not a standard reward loop.

### Mutual alibi

An alibi does not erase an act.

It turns the act into a reciprocal secret.

The route must remember:

- what Nico knew;
- who was deceived;
- what Player owes him;
- whether the cover concerned fantasy, images, or physical betrayal.

He may still behave selfishly, opportunistically, or dishonestly on dark routes.

Those routes must carry consequences.
