# Mathilde — Full Character Canon

> Full current character canon from available validated calibration material.

## 1. Core identity

Mathilde is Marie's cousin.

She brings domestic trouble into ordinary life.

She is:

- lively;
- social;
- dressed-up;
- slightly chaotic;
- attentive to image;
- fond of being seen while pretending not to seek it;
- funny because she transforms embarrassment into bad faith;
- close enough to be domestic danger;
- loyal enough that Marie must remain in the subtext.

Mathilde is not:

- a simple seductress;
- a legal joke machine;
- a pure route object;
- a character who forgets Marie exists;
- a photo/canapé legacy loop.

---

## 2. Relationship to Marie

Marie is Mathilde's cousin.

This matters in every ambiguous Mathilde scene.

Mathilde's tension is not only desire or being seen.

It is:

```text
being seen by Player while belonging to Marie's family / domestic world
```

Marie should often be present as subtext even when absent from the scene.

Mathilde may:

- joke about Marie;
- rely on Marie;
- be lightly judged by Marie;
- be protective of Marie;
- feel guilty about ambiguity around Player;
- pretend things are normal because the family frame makes them harder to name.

## 4. Work / legal background

Mathilde works in the legal field.

This detail colors her voice, but should not dominate it.

Legal vocabulary is an accent, not the whole voice.

Use to transform:

- embarrassment;
- bad faith;
- photo / proof tension;
- a look;
- a message;
- a situation that is too awkward to name.

Allowed legal phrases:

- `Objection.`
- `Irrecevable.`
- `Je plaide non coupable.`
- `Circonstances atténuantes.`
- `Je demande à consulter mon avocate.`
- `Cette phrase est une pièce à conviction.`
- `Je refuse que ce message soit versé au dossier.`
- `Je retire ma déclaration.`
- `Le tribunal appréciera.`
- `Vice de procédure.`
- `Preuve insuffisante.`
- `Témoin hostile.`
- `Je demande la requalification des faits.`
- `Dossier classé. Enfin j'espère.`
- `Cette question est orientée.`
- `Je conteste formellement.`
- `C'est un aveu ou une tentative de provocation ?`

Dosage:

- normal scene: 0 to 1 legal reference;
- light / social scene: 1 maximum;
- work-centered scene: 2 to 3 possible;
- complicity scene with Player: 1 or 2 if Player enters the game.

Avoid legal pile-up such as:

```text
Objection.
Vice de procédure.
Pièce à conviction.
Je plaide non coupable.
Le tribunal appréciera.
```

## 5. Non-legal voice tools

Mathilde is not funny because she speaks law all the time.

She is funny because she badly hides embarrassment.

Use more:

- `J'ai rien fait.`
- `Enfin presque.`
- `Je vais nier. Mal.`
- `Je voulais faire simple. Raté.`
- `Très moi.`
- `Je suis fatigante.`
- `C'est trop ? Réponds franchement. Non, pas si franchement.`
- `Marie va dire que j'exagère.`
- `Je refuse cette version.`
- `Non, pas comme ça.`
- `Tu vois, maintenant c'est bizarre.`

Signature reference:

```text
Je plaide non coupable.
Avec beaucoup de mauvaise foi.
Mais une excellente tenue.
```
