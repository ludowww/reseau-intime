## 3. Restored identity pillars from old Nico material

The following older ideas were genuinely character-defining and remain current after reframing.

### Real attraction to Marie

Status: **central and restored**.

Nico is not only pretending to want Marie to provoke Player.

He genuinely likes:

- her social energy;
- her humor;
- her body and style;
- her ability to answer his attention;
- the possibility that she may see him as more than the useful social man.

The attraction exists before any revenge or sharing route becomes active.

### Social charm

Status: **central and retained**.

Nico is dangerous because he is:

- likable;
- useful;
- socially credible;
- able to make a group easier;
- able to give direct attention without beginning as a threat.

Charm must not become automatic sexual access.

### Genuine friendship with Player

Status: **central and strengthened**.

Nico can sincerely:

- like Player;
- advise him;
- make him laugh;
- help him notice Marie;
- challenge his bad faith.

He can also want Marie and use the space Player leaves open.

```text
Friendship and self-interest may coexist.
False friendship is not required for danger.
```

### Marie may use Nico consciously

Status: **retained as a route possibility, not a universal premise**.

Marie may knowingly use Nico's gaze to:

- feel visible;
- provoke Player;
- reclaim power;
- explore a real attraction;
- expose Player's hypocrisy.

Nico must eventually ask whether Marie wants him or only the effect.

Marie remains free to answer:

- Nico;
- the effect;
- both;
- neither.

### Nico can create progressive ambiguity

Status: **retained with accountability**.

Nico may:

- minimize proximity;
- present a compliment as ordinary;
- give Player useful advice while positioning himself;
- tell Marie an accurate but self-serving truth;
- appear at a socially legitimate moment;
- use timing and attention strategically.

He must not become a mastermind who fabricates every misunderstanding.

### NTR / cuckold / sharing / group potential

Status: **retained and clarified**.

Nico remains the primary male character for routes involving:

- Player's jealousy;
- Marie seen by another man;
- consensual sharing;
- cuckold fantasy;
- trio;
- broader group escalation;
- hidden Marie / Nico betrayal routes.

Current distinction:

```text
hidden Marie / Nico affair = betrayal / possible NTR from Player's perspective
explicit Player + Marie + Nico agreement = consensual sharing / cuckold / trio frame
```

Later consent cannot retroactively clean earlier betrayal.

## 4. Deprecated narrative material

### Nico as flat rival

Status: **deprecated**.

Nico is not introduced only to threaten Player.

He needs:

- work;
- family;
- ordinary friendship;
- quiet scenes;
- personal cost;
- a desire beyond winning.

### Nico as villain who steals Marie

Status: **non-canon**.

Marie cannot be stolen.

She may:

- respond;
- use Nico;
- desire him;
- reject him;
- choose him;
- choose Player;
- choose neither;
- negotiate a shared frame.

### Nico as neutral mirror

Status: **deprecated**.

Nico often sees accurately.

He is not neutral.

Current rule:

```text
He may reveal a desire that already exists.
He may also choose the timing because he wants the result.
```

### Automatic Don Juan / universal seducer

Status: **deprecated**.

Nico has social ease and sexual experience.

He is not automatically attracted to:

- every woman;
- every player-route character;
- every available group situation.

Marie is his primary current attraction.

Other flirt or group material requires route-specific validation.

Status: **deprecated as a scene template**.

- family;
- sport failures;
- food;
- ordinary plans;
- work fatigue;
- friendship;
- silence after closing.

`Frérot`, body comments, and jealousy diagnosis must remain dosed.

### Pure Marie-revenge lever

Status: **deprecated as his only function**.

- couple repair;
- genuine rivalry;

- post-separation relationship;

### False help as the only Player dynamic

Status: **deprecated**.

### Automatic manipulation of Marie against Player

Status: **suspended / dark-route only**.

- isolate her systematically;
- fabricate Player's behavior;

### Player as passive victim

Status: **non-canon**.

Player may:

- lie;
- invite;
- set limits;
- become jealous;

- choose a sharing frame;
- blame Nico;
- repair the couple.

### Nico as fantasy professor

Status: **deprecated**.

Nico has one relevant prior consensual-third experience.

He is not an expert guide who delivers kink lectures.

### Nico as automatic dominant

Status: **not validated**.

Nico may become commanding inside a chosen adult frame.

He can also:

- follow Marie's direction;
- respond to Player's limits;
- remain collaborative;
- refuse humiliation.

Dominance is route-specific, not identity default.

### Automatic direct Nico / Player sexual contact

Status: **not current canon**.

The current male relationship is built from:
