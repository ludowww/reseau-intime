# Sandra / Player — Address Formula Addendum

> Documentation-only writing addendum.  
> Complements `docs/Character_Playable_Voice_Cards.md` and the Sandra sections of the character voice references.  
> No runtime, narrative JSON, tests, assets, or playable content are changed.

## Decision

The address pair:

```text
Sandra : mon cher Player
Player : ma chère Sandra
```

is a validated private code between Sandra and Player.

It is not a regular nickname. It is a rare, playful mirror formula: false politeness, hidden tenderness, and light disguised seduction.

## Function

The formula can be used to:

- suggest an older, already-established complicity;
- soften a sentence that is a little too precise;
- hide a compliment inside formal politeness;
- make a flirtatious beat feel elegant rather than frontal;
- show that Player can answer Sandra with her own rhythm;
- make the exchange feel less generic and more private.

## Usage rules

Use sparingly.

Recommended maximum:

```text
One or two uses in an important Sandra / Player scene.
Never as an automatic nickname.
```

The formula is stronger when Player mirrors Sandra after she used it first.

Good placement:

- after a photo;
- after a light tease;
- after a too-accurate sentence;
- after a `haha` that opens the door;
- when one of them wants to hide tenderness behind a polite form.

Bad usage:

- every scene;
- every greeting;
- as a fixed pet name;
- as a substitute for real dialogue;
- in moments where Sandra is setting a serious boundary.

## Examples

```text
Sandra : Tu deviens très pénible, mon cher Player.

Player : J’essaie de rester à la hauteur, ma chère Sandra.

Sandra : Attention.
Sandra : Là, tu joues avec mes propres armes.
```

```text
Player : Je vais faire semblant de croire au hasard du cadrage.

Sandra : Très aimable, mon cher Player.

Player : Je suis d’une grande délicatesse, ma chère Sandra.

Sandra : Faux.
Sandra : Mais joliment formulé.
```

```text
Sandra : Tu es très pénible, mon cher Player.

Player : Et toi très dangereuse, ma chère Sandra.

Sandra : Voilà.
Sandra : C’est exactement pour ce genre de phrase que je devrais faire attention.
```

## Guardrail

This code should feel like a small shared smile, not a mechanical catchphrase.

If the formula appears, it must carry a small emotional or flirtatious function in that exact moment.
