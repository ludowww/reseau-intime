## Main canon decisions

- Nicolas, called Nico, is 34 and explicitly adult.
- He is floor manager at `L'Annexe`, a local bar-restaurant with a terrace and small events.
- He is an employee, not a glamorous venue owner.
- His work includes reservations, staff coordination, difficult customers, small events, closing, and occasional collaboration with La Verrière.
- He is often sober while others drink and must recognize intoxication as incompatible with valid adult consent.
- He lives alone in a small one-bedroom apartment near L'Annexe.
- His apartment is quiet and practical rather than a permanent party space.
- His older sister Julie is an ordinary family anchor and one of the few people who can stop his performance with a direct question.
- Nico was in a five-year relationship with Clara and lived with her for around three years.
- The relationship ended around eighteen months before the game.
- Nico did not maintain a hidden physical affair with Clara.
- The relationship failed partly because everyone else received his attentive social version while private intimacy received his fatigue and avoidance.
- He is single at game start.
- He has adult sexual experience but is not a conquest collector or infallible seducer.
