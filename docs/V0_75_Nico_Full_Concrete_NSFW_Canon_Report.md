## Image-consent levels

### Level 1 — Public / social

- already public or intended for a group;
- no private or adult permission inferred.

### Level 2 — Chosen sexy / shared gaze

- subject knows the sexual or flirtatious purpose;
- audience is named;
- saving and forwarding rules are clear;
- valid adult reward.

### Level 3 — Unauthorized private circulation

- non-explicit private image;
- second audience was not approved;

- stolen, secretly captured, or forwarded against a clear rule;
- serious harm;
- positive routes may close;
- confrontation, deletion, rupture, or dark consequences are required.
