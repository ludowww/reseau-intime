## Previous relationship and wound

Raphaëlle was in a six-year relationship with Étienne and lived with him for around three years.

The relationship ended a little over two years before the game.

Étienne did not physically abuse her and is not a convenient villain.

- the rules functioned partly as retroactive permission.

```text

```

This confirms her distinction:

```text

A vague relationship is not automatically freer.
```
