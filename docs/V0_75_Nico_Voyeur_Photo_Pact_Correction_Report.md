## Consent matrix

### Level A — authorized shared gaze

The photographed adult knows the image exists, knows the recipients and erotic purpose, and defines saving or forwarding rules.

This can be a clean adult reward.

### Level B — unapproved sexual recontextualization or circulation

The image may be legitimately possessed, but the photographed adult did not authorize sexualization, recropping, forwarding, saving, or the new recipient.

This is a dark betrayal route with secrecy, guilt, discovery risk, and character-specific consequences.

```text
Permission to possess an image
is not permission to sexualize, recrop, or forward it.
```

### Level C — prohibited capture / severe harm

Hidden cameras, bathroom or under-clothing capture, sleeping/incapacitated capture, secret recording of sex, stolen explicit images, and coercive threats are not a standard reward loop.

If such content appears at all, it belongs only to a severe negative route with immediate instability and major consequences.
