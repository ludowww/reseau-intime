## 2. Product-owner correction

The project is now explicitly reaffirmed as:

```text
an adult visual narrative game
where photos are the primary player reward
and route progression promises character-specific pornography
```

The game is not designed as:

- a mostly textual erotic novel;
- a restrained relationship drama with rare illustrations;
- a gallery of rooms, objects, or neutral traces;
- a sequence where every risky route is postponed in the name of caution.

The story, dialogue, and psychology exist to make the visual access, sexual escalation, fantasy identity, betrayal, jealousy, and adult consequences stronger.

- chosen cheating;
- hidden betrayal;
- voyeuristic complicity;
- unauthorized recontextualization;
- consensual shared gaze;

The player must see:


## 9. Choice-design correction

The repeated template:

```text
respectful
playful
withdrawn
```

is insufficient for an adult visual game.

Choices must now create visibly different image and route outcomes.

Examples:

- ask for the private version;
- tell Mathilde exactly what her pose does to Player;
- ask Sandra for one more chosen image;
- encourage Marie to wear the dress where others see her;
- ask Pauline whether she made another crop;
- accept Nico's sexualized selection;
- refuse image circulation;
- ask Raphaëlle to show the transformation process;
- choose a private couple continuation.

The risky opportunity must be readable.

The refusal must remain possible.

The reward must not be identical across all options.

## 10. Narrative naturalness correction

Frequent photos do not require long justifications.

Use a simple sequence:

```text
concrete situation
-> visible woman
-> image
-> desire or hesitation
-> meaningful choice
-> unlock / withheld image / risk
```

Do not make every character deliver a polished relationship thesis before showing anything.

The image should carry much of the story.

Character-specific triggers:

- Marie gets ready and wants Player to react;
- Mathilde asks about an outfit and realizes she wants his gaze;
- Sandra chooses an extra image after hesitation;
- Pauline controls public and private versions;
- Raphaëlle reveals process and transformation;
- Nico changes the audience and erotic interpretation.
